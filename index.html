Common Questions

Straight answers. No jargon, no upsell.

Do you charge for emergency calls after hours?
We charge the same flat rate 24/7. No "after-hours surcharge" or weekend markup. The only difference is how fast we get there.
How fast can you get here for an emergency?
Average response is 30–45 minutes inside our primary service area. We'll give you an exact ETA when you call.
Are your quotes really flat-rate?
Yes. After we diagnose the issue, we quote one price for the complete repair. If we find something unexpected, we stop and ask before proceeding.
Do you warranty your work?
Every repair carries a 1-year workmanship warranty. Parts carry the manufacturer warranty. If something we fixed fails, we come back at no charge.
Can you work with my insurance?
Absolutely. We document everything with photos and detailed invoices. We've worked with every major homeowner's insurance carrier in the region.
